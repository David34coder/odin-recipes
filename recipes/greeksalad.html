Greek salad

This Greek salad recipe is incredible good! It's nice and tangy and even better in teh summer when you use fresh vegetables.

Greek salad is often imagined as a farmer's breakfast or lunch, as its ingredients

resemble those that a Greek farmer might have on hand.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 head romaine lettuce-rinsed, dried and chopped
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 2 large tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 (6ounce) can pitted black olives
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 6 tablespoons live oil
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • ground black pepper to taste

    • DIRECTIONS

    Step 1

    combine romaine, cucumber, tomatoes, llives, bell peppers, and red onion in a large bowl; sprinkle with feta cheese.

    Step 2

    whisk olive oil, lemon juice, oregano, and black pepper together in a small bowl pour dressing over salad, toss well to combine, and serve.
